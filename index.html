About TEDx SJEC

TEDxSJEC is a platform that brings together curious, creative, and progressive thinkers from St. Joseph Engineering College to have a conversation about this year's theme: Ember.
Featuring a dynamic group of speakers from diverse backgrounds and experiences who aim to impart inspiration and intellectual acceleration to young adults, enabling them to rekindle the dying fire and open up a world of opportunity by taking these students on a thought-provoking journey.

EMBER

“Small things make a big difference”, is something we've heard time and again. An ember, per Oxford English Dictionary, is defined as "a small piece of burning or glowing coal or wood in a dying fire". It may sometimes be easy to lose hope in life, and the struggles may seem unending; But even when hardships tower over you and the times get dark, One must remember to always look for the light. Ember urges us to be that last ray of hope amidst the darkness for ourselves and others around us.
